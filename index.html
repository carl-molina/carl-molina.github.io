ABOUT ME


Hi, my name's Carl!

I'm a filmmaker turned software engineer.

For the last five years, my day job was a Field Operator Specialist for autonomous vehicle operations. By night, I was a Bay Area filmmaker writing and directing films, and winning some awards along the way! My passion for computers and software was always there, though: from taking Intro to Comp Sci in highschool, Modern Web Apps in college, and taking CS courses at De Anza College, I love everything about coding. Now, I've decided to pursue this passion as a career in order to contribute more as a software engineer on the other side of test and development. You can find me deep in grinding Codewars, journaling in cafes around the Greater SF Bay Area, or hiking trails on the peninsula.

Currently studying in Rithm School's Full Stack Software Engineer Bootcamp November 2023 cohort. Let's grab coffee! (I just recently started my coffee journey--if you have any recommendations, please let me know!!)


Some fun facts about me:

  • I love visiting new places, like Japan and NYC this year!
  • I take my own photos and try to include them in most of my content.
  • My last short film won Best Upcoming Filmmaker and was featured on NBC Bay Area.
  • My grandparents had 16 kids! Since I was 10, I've been documenting their immigrant, Philippines-American journey through each of their children. Decades from now, I hope to publish all the video interviews and stories I've collected in order to preserve my grandparents' history and legacy.
  • Mochi and Matcha are my dogs!
